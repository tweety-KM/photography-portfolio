Clouds

An exploration of atmosphere and scale through cloud motifs and lighting.

Context & Brief

This series examines natural textures and the emotional weight of sky.

Lighting

Layered light was used to sculpt depth and reveal texture in the clouds.

Composition

Framing choices focused on negative space to accentuate mood.

These images aim to translate ephemeral weather into considered visual statements.
